### Key Points:
- **strncpy:** This function copies up to `MAX_LEN - 1` characters from the source string to ensure that the destination buffer is terminated with a null character, avoiding a buffer overflow.
- **Null Check:** Before accessing `clienthp->h_name`, there is a check to ensure `clienthp` is not `NULL`, which prevents dereferencing a null pointer if `gethostbyaddr` fails.
- **Zero-out Buffer:** The call to `memset` is not strictly necessary, but it ensures that the `hostname` buffer is zeroed out beforehand. This may be useful for security purposes by ensuring that the entire buffer does not retain old or uninitialized data.
